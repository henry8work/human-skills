# 7 — Others

Drop here anything that doesn't fit in the previous folders but helps understand the brand:

- Old deck (even if outdated)
- Draft brand manual
- Word doc with loose ideas
- Short business plan
- Old briefings that worked well
- Podcast link whose tone represents what you're after
- Spotify playlist that defines the emotional mood
- Movie/series that captures the brand's "vibe"
- Photograph of a physical space (store, atelier, studio)
- Book/poetry page that says something about the brand

No format rule — anything works. The more material, the better the briefing.
