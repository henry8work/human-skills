# 3 — Visual references

Drop here any image that defines the **visual mood** you want for your brand.

**It can be anything:**
- Pinterest, Behance, Are.na screenshot
- Magazine cover that stuck with you (a photo of the cover works)
- Screenshot of a website whose design you love
- A photo of yours that has the right mood
- Instagram screenshot of a brand you admire
- Page from a book/zine
- Any image that makes you say "that's it"

**The more the better — but 5-10 images are enough.** You don't need a huge library. Prefer 5 images that truly represent you over 50 that vaguely remind of your style.

**Tip:** mix types. It doesn't have to all be the same genre. A magazine cover + a website screenshot + a film still + a zine page = great briefing.

**About grids:** if you have a feed screenshot (Behance, Instagram) with several examples in a single image, it counts as a reference too. I can mentally decompose it.

**What you DON'T need to add:**
- Images from your current brand (those go in `4-exemplos-do-meu-tom/`)
- Anti-examples (those go in `6-anti-referencias/`)
