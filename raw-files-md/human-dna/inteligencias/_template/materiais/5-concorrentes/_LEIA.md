# 5 — Competitors

Drop here screenshots, links or any material from brands that occupy the **same space** as you.

**What it's for:** understand what the market is already doing, identify what you want to do **differently**, and map the "white space" — things nobody in the category is doing well.

**It's not to copy.** It's for contrast.

**It can be:**
- Screenshot of the competitor's Instagram feed
- Website screenshot
- Logo + a few pieces
- Link to their profile (paste into a `.txt` file)

**Ideal quantity:** 2-3 competitors. More than that becomes noise.

**Tip:** choose the **strongest competitor** (not the weakest). A useful comparison is with a high reference — "what does this category leader do that we are going to do differently?".
