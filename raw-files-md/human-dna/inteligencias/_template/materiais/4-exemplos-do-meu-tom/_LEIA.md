# 4 — Examples of my tone

Drop here texts of yours that represent well **how your brand speaks**. This will help me calibrate the voice to sound like yours, not like some generic brand.

**It can be:**
- Instagram caption of yours that you liked the result of
- Email you sent that turned out well-written
- Excerpt from your website (about, hero, any page)
- A DM you replied to that landed in the right voice
- Tweet, thread, LinkedIn post
- Screenshot of any text of yours

**Format:** `.txt`, `.md`, `.rtf`, or screenshot (PNG/JPG). You can also paste text directly into the chat later — any path works.

**Ideal quantity:** 3-5 different examples. If possible, examples from different channels (a caption, an email, a website text) — it helps me understand how the tone modulates.

**Bonus:** if you have **a text of yours that does NOT represent the right tone** (something that turned out generic, too formal, or off-brand), drop it in too with `RUIM-` at the start of the name (e.g. `RUIM-email-template.txt`). This helps me understand what to avoid as much as what to aim for.
